About Me

Making Complex Products
Feel Simple.

I'm a UI/UX designer at Hyundai HT, where I lead the design system for smart home wallpad interfaces deployed across residential and commercial buildings in Korea. My focus is building scalable systems that support complex device ecosystems while keeping every interaction simple and predictable.

Before that, I spent three years at Data B as the sole designer on an AI-powered English writing platform, owning the product end-to-end: UX flows, UI design, and brand identity. The redesign increased user retention by 21%.

My background in industrial design shapes how I approach digital products. I think about interfaces in terms of physical interaction: where attention lands, how people move through a system, and what feedback they expect at each step. That means reducing flows to their essential decisions and building systems that scale without adding noise.

I work closely with PMs, engineers, and marketers across the full product lifecycle. I care about how design drives business outcomes, and I bring cross-team input into the process so every design decision moves the product forward.
